Working Process of Fest

Here's your guide to Cicada 2022 process. Go through all the steps to know the exact process of the fest.

22 October-15 November, 2022

Registration

Pick your favourite event(s) and register in that event by filling the form corresponding to that event. Its that easy :)

16-17 November, 2022

Participation

Participate offline. The details for your registered events will be sent to you via email and whatsapp groups. Use those details and show your talent.

4:00 PM, 16 November, 2022

Hackathon Begins

An open hackathon is going to start , Onboarding will start from 3:00 PM

4:00 PM 17 November, 2022

Hackathon Ends

The hackathon ends here and the prizes will be distributed in the valedictory function,

17 November, 2022

Result Declaration

The ultimate genius will be revealed by our judging panel on 17th November, 2022 and the results will be announced on the Whatsapp groups and will be mailed to you.

Shortly after result declaration

Prize Distribution

The winners will be contacted by our team to collect thier prizes in the valedictory function. Enjoy your prizes :)
